Introducing Andrew Watson

Andrew is the owner, operator and journeyman plumber for AW Plumbing. He has been serving Oregon's plumbing needs for more than a decade.

Andrew's Mission Statement

Our mission is simple:

To be the plumber you can truly depend on. We provide honest, high-quality plumbing services with respect for your time, your home, and your budget. As a family-owned business, integrity isn't just a word - it's how we operate every day.

More about Andrew and AW Plumbing

Andrew and his family have recently relocated to Grants Pass from Salem. He and his wife Taylor, have three beautiful children, Mila, Micah and Aleah. The entire family enjoys snowboarding and they love spending time on the mountain together. They made the move to Grants Pass in order to be closer to family. In essessence they have come home. Andrew has years of education and experience, which led to his journeyman plumber status. With the support of his family, he has been able to hone his craft and perfect his trade.
